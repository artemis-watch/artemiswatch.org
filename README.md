# Artemis Watch

Software that identifies victims and exposes perpetrators of CSAM.

## Solutions

* CSCAN: is a developer-friendly API for real-time or batch content screening. CSCAN is an offline-first, AI-powered scanning system built to detect and flag suspected CSAM in both images and videos - before it reaches your users, your infrastructure, or your compliance boundary. Designed for easy integration into any upload or ingestion pipeline, CSCAN supports transactional or batch processing, leverages parallel execution for speed, and provides reliable, resumable analysis at scale - all with minimal latency and zero cloud dependencies.

* Sift: monitor's cert transparency logs for over 24 transparency log providers, allowing you to create keyword-based alerts to identify potential CSAM-related domains and subdomains. Sift provides a powerful tool for proactive monitoring and early detection of CSAM-related activities in the digital landscape.
  * Sift has an optional DGA module that can be used to identify potential DGA-generated domains, which are often used by malicious actors to evade detection. By analyzing domain patterns and behaviors, the DGA module can help identify suspicious domains that may be associated with CSAM activities.

* Orion: an automated OSINT tool that takes in photos and videos, extracts metadata and landmarks, and provides a map of where the photo or video was taken, helping investigators identify potential locations of someone who may be in danger.

* Deep Crawl: Intelligent Metadata and Keyword Scanning Across the Clearnet and Darknet. Deep Crawl continuously scans the clearnet and thousands of TOR domains to detect high-risk, exploitative content using advanced keyword analysis, metadata extraction, and DNS pattern recognition. Our system identifies emerging threats by analyzing historical domain behavior, registrar data, and hosting patterns. Combined with our proprietary neural network, Deep Crawl flags domains likely involved in CSAM distribution - even before they're fully indexed. Export flagged domains as blocklists or integrate them directly into your content filtering infrastructure to proactively shield your platform from harmful content.

* Dashboard: a user-friendly interface that provides real-time insights and analytics on the data collected by our tools. The dashboard allows users to visualize trends, track flagged content, and manage alerts effectively, empowering them to take informed actions against CSAM-related activities.
