CSCAN is a developer-friendly API for real-time or batch content screening

CSCAN is an offline-first, AI-powered scanning system built to detect and flag suspected CSAM in both images and videos - before it reaches your users, your infrastructure, or your compliance boundary. Designed for easy integration into any upload or ingestion pipeline, CSCAN supports transactional or batch processing, leverages parallel execution for speed, and provides reliable, resumable analysis at scale - all with minimal latency and zero cloud dependencies.

Deep Crawl: Intelligent Metadata and Keyword Scanning Across the Clearnet and Darknet

Deep Crawl continuously scans the clearnet and thousands of TOR domains to detect high-risk, exploitative content using advanced keyword analysis, metadata extraction, and DNS pattern recognition. Our system identifies emerging threats by analyzing historical domain behavior, registrar data, and hosting patterns. Combined with our proprietary neural network, Deep Crawl flags domains likely involved in CSAM distribution - even before they're fully indexed. Export flagged domains as blocklists or integrate them directly into your content filtering infrastructure to proactively shield your platform from harmful content.
